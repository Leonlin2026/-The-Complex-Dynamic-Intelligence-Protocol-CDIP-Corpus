Abstract

One potential. Every component.

KUT is a transformer architecture in which attention, residual transport, normalization, the MLP, the readout head, and the training loss are all derived from a single quartic Kähler potential K = (α/2)|z|⁴ + β|z|². Setting α → 0 exactly recovers the standard Transformer. Training a 578M-parameter model from a Qwen3 donor on 450M tokens of WikiText and FineMath grows α by 13× — cross-entropy gradient descent actively discovers curved geometry.

▸ One potential generates everything. Metric, connection, curvature, quantization scale ℏ = π/ω, vacuum mass, attractor forces, and section readout all derive from K — no hand-tuned geometric weights, no separate temperature, no ad-hoc regularizers.

▸ Causally safe and FlashAttention-compatible. Attention reduces to ⟨R(Γ)q, k⟩ — a single matmul with a connection-induced rotation. Three locks (Γ in attention, g in MLP/norm, g·ℏ in readout) prevent geometry-bypass shortcuts; verified by 25/25 SP/AR agreement.

▸ Generation works. The flat limit (α → 0) is exact at the architecture level. Training drives α from 0.05 to 0.65 — a 13× growth — while ℏω = π is preserved to four decimal places. PPL drops from 96 (WikiText, 100M) to 43 (FineMath, 450M) with coherent text and math generation.

01

α direction is the architectural health indicator

If α grows during training, geometry is necessary — the model is using the manifold structure. If α decays, the model is destroying geometry to take a shortcut. KUT v13.3 grows α from 0.049 to 0.654 on FineMath — a 13× increase — while loss decreases monotonically. The geometric channel is doing real work.

02

Bohr–Sommerfeld quantization holds at the architecture level

The identity ℏω = π is enforced as a mathematical constraint, not as a soft penalty. Across 450M tokens of training, the empirical product stays within four decimal places of π. The quantization scale ℏ is not a learned hyperparameter — it is a derivable consequence of the geometry.

03

Section readout is necessary; the linear head degenerates

Ablation comparing the geometric section Ψ = m·R(θ)·h against a standard linear head (with backbone identical) reveals that the linear head produces only token salad ("narburyularityory…") while the section produces coherent prose and recognizable mathematical notation. The geometry-to-symbol bridge is the section, not the matrix.

04

FlashAttention compatibility, zero accuracy compromise

The full Hermitian attention (π/ℏ)·⟨R(Γ)q, k⟩/√d reduces to a single matmul of R(Γ)q against k — directly compatible with the FlashAttention kernel. The connection rotation R(Γ) is a per-head 2×2 transform that absorbs the entire symplectic structure into the queries, no kernel modification needed.
